About us

Welcome to UNIQU, your number one source for creative ink doodle arts. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of illustrations, with an emphasis on wildness, creativity and out-side-the-box thinking.
Founded in 2022 by NHLP, UNIQU has come a long way from its beginnings in Viet Nam. When NHLP first started out, His passion for unique doodles inking drove them to start their own business.
